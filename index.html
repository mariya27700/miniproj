About

MPK hospital is one of the leading healthcare institutions in North Kerala, committed to delivering world-class medical services to the people. With a focus on patient-centered care, the hospital provides a wide range of medical treatments, advanced diagnostic services, and sophisticated healthcare solutions in a comfortable, safe, and modern environment. The hospital's commitment to excellence has made it a trusted name in the region, catering to patients from all walks of life and providing top-notch healthcare services at affordable costs.

Mission

To provide high-quality, affordable healthcare with a focus on patient-centered care, clinical excellence, and compassion, ensuring access for all.

Vision

To be a trusted leader in healthcare, continuously evolving with advanced technology and innovation to offer the best possible medical services.
